India has an incredible history, and all the Indian states have their own heritage. Some of the best rulers of history have ruled in India, especially in Maharashtra. Maharashtra has a history of the rule of Mughals and Marathas, who had large regions under their control. During the period of their rule, they have built some of the magnificent arts, architecture, and forts. There are many renowned forts in Maharashtra built by different rulers; however, most of these were built under the reign of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The forts of Maharashtra are architectural marvels with a profound history that describes the life of people during the time of the establishment of these forts.

1 Lohagadh Fort Located in Lonavala, Lohagadh was one of the strongholds used by Shivaji Maharaj for military defense.
2 Janjira Fort Located in Murud, this fort was one of the strongest marine forts in the region, with a rich history of maritime warfare.
3 Daulatabad Fort Situated in Daulatabad, this fort is known for its massive fortifications and strategic location.
4 Panhala Fort Located in Panhala, it played a key role in the Maratha Empire's defense and was the site of many important events during Shivaji's reign.
5 Raigadh Fort Situated in Raigadh, this fort is most famous as the coronation place of Shivaji Maharaj.
6 Shanivarwada Fort Located in Pune, it was the seat of the Peshwa rulers of the Maratha Empire before it was destroyed by fire.
7 Yashwantgarh Fort Located in Redi, this fort is relatively less known but still holds strategic importance.
8 Sindhugarh Fort Located in Malvan, it was used by the Marathas for controlling the coastal trade routes.
9 Sinhagadh Fort This fort in Pune is famous for the Battle of Sinhagad, a key battle in the Maratha history.
10 Pratapgad Fort Situated in Satara, it was the site of the famous Battle of Pratapgad between Shivaji and Bijapur forces.
11 Torna Fort Located in Pune, Torna was one of the first forts captured by Shivaji in 1643.
12 Kandhar Fort Located in Nanded, this fort was an important part of the Maratha defense in the Deccan region.
13 Rajmachi Fort Located in Pune, it is a popular trekking destination and offers a beautiful view of the surrounding areas.
14 Tikona Fort Located in Lonavala, this fort is known for its triangular shape and picturesque surroundings.
15 Vasota Fort This fort in Satara is known for its steep terrain and the surrounding dense forest.
16 Visapur Fort Located in Malavali, Visapur Fort is one of the larger forts built by the Marathas to defend their territories.
17 Suvarnadurg Fort Located in Dapoli, this fort was an important maritime fort during the time of Shivaji Maharaj.
18 Korigad Fort Located in Aambi Valley, it offers scenic views of the surrounding hills and a glimpse into the Maratha fort architecture.
19 Akola Fort Located in Akola District, it is one of the important forts built during Shivaji Maharaj's reign.
20 Vasai Fort Situated in Vasai-Virar, this fort was used for coastal defense and is now a popular tourist attraction.
21 Prabalgad Fort Located in Raigad District, it was part of the Maratha military defense network.
22 Harishchandragad Fort Located in Ahmednagar District, this fort is known for its ancient temples and historic significance.
23 Mandangad Fort Located in Ratnagiri District, it is a lesser-known fort but has a unique military structure.
24 Vijaydurg Fort Situated in Vijaydurg, it is a coastal fort with a rich maritime history and strategic importance.
